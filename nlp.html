Natural Language Processing CS6314- NIT Rourkela

Prerequisites

  • Theory: Linear Algebra, Analytic Geometry, Matrix decompositions, Vector Calculus, Probability and Distributions, Continuous Optimization. Refer Chapters 1-7 from the book `https://mml-book.github.io/book/mml-book.pdf`
  • Programming: Python, Source: https://www.w3schools.com/python/default.asp,
    Pytorch\tensorflow, Source: https://pytorch.org/assets/deep-learning/Deep-Learning-with-PyTorch.pdf, https://github.com/gigwegbe/Learning-TensorFlow, TF book
  • Software and Packages: Conda, Jupyter notebook, NLTK - https://www.nltk.org/book/

Books

The Majority of the course content follows from the first two references.
  • Jacob Eisenstein. Natural Language Processing.
  • Dan Jurafsky and James H. Martin. Speech and Language Processing (3rd ed. draft).
  • Christopher Manning and Hinrich Schutze. Foundations of Statistical Natural Language Processing.
