Welcome to the Short Term Course in Natural Language Processing

Date: June 10th-14th, 2024

Location: Department of CSE, NIT Rourkela, Odisha, India, 769008

Schedule

Day 1: Introduction to Natural Language Processing

  • Text processing
  • Word Embedding
  • Text Classification

Day 2: Transformers

  • Basic architecture
  • Application to Text Classification
  • Sequence labelling with LSTM

Day 3: Machine Translation, Summarization

  • Basics of SMT, NMT and implementations
  • Basics of Abstractive and Extractive Summarization and implementations

Day 4: Question-Answering System, Chatbot Design

  • Designing a QA system, specifications, training, decoding and implementations
  • Designing Chatbots efficiently using pretrained models and models developed from scratch

Day 5: Language Models

  • Introduction to Probabilistic Language models with NLTK implementation
  • Design considerations of state of the art models like ChatGPT.
  • Implementation of Neural Language Models
