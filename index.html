I am working as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the National Institute of Technology (NIT) Rourkela. I obtained my Bachelor of Engineering (BE) degree from VSSUT Burla, Sambalpur in 2010 and Master of Technology (M Tech) and Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.), both from IIT Kharagpur, in 2013 and 2017, respectively. I joined National Institute of Technology, Rourkela in February 2018. I am serving as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science for the last 6 years. My primary interesets are Combinatorics and Graph Theory. I have received a project under the MATRICS scheme from SERB in this direction in December 2023. Moreover, I have broadened my research in the directions of Natural Language Processing. I have undertaken and completed one project from Meity, Govt. of India. I have three Ph.D scholars (two scholars have graduated, one has submitted her thesis). I have also supervised more than 20 undergraduates and more than 15 masters students.
